About the Swimming Carnival

The 2025 College Swimming Carnival was held on Tuesday 18th February at Botany Aquatic Centre. Students in the championship events started at 8am and the competition was fierce. After the championship events had concluded, all students began on a rotation system of activities to earn house points. After a successful carnival, the winning house was CHANEL. Congratulations to all students who are in Chanel and winning the House Championship for 2025.
